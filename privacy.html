HNPS Band Attendance

Privacy Policy

What data do we collect?

This app collects a small amount of information to track band attendance:

  • Student first names
  • Instrument played
  • Attendance records (present/absent per session)

Who can access the data?

Only band coordinators and parent volunteers who have the app link can view or record attendance. There are no public-facing pages or user accounts.

How is the data stored?

Data is stored in a Supabase-hosted database located in Sydney, Australia. The connection between the app and the database is encrypted (HTTPS).

Do we share data with third parties?

No. Student data is never sold, shared, or provided to any third party. The data is used solely for tracking band attendance at the school.

How long is data kept?

Attendance data is kept for the duration of the current school year. At the end of the year, or at a coordinator's request, all data can be deleted.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or would like data removed, please contact the HNPS Band Coordinator through the school office.

Effective date: April 2026
